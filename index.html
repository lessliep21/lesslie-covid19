How to keep yourself busy during a quarantine 101

EXERCISE

Working out does not only keep you busy but it also helps keep you in shape and doesn't make your pockets hurt!

COOKING

This is a great way to bond with your family, learn to cook new foods and boom you are a chef!.

READ A BOOK

This is a good time to pick up any book of your choosing and dive into a new world. This also helps build your knowledge.

MEDITATION

During a time like this you can imagine everyone stressed, so take this time to meditate and to relax your mind, soul, and body.
